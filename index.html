Welcome

Mogalakwena Men's Relate (herein referred as MMR) is an organization which aims to promote unity amongst men in Mogalakwena and promote social cohesion thus addressing community social ills.

The name of the organization shall be called Mogalakwena Men's Relate (MMR).

Purpose

The purpose of this organization is to stand against amongst others; Social ills, GBV, Communicable disease, Non-communicable disease, Child abuse, Poverty alleviation, Inequality, Unemployment, Illiteracy, Crime, Juvenile delinquency, Illicit drugs. Embark on programmes aimed at Community Development and Empowerment.

Vision

Mogalakwena Men's Relate strives to make men aware of all social ills within the community and create a conducive environment where all people should live in peace and harmony.

Mission

Create a conducive environment within communities and ensuring that people would be able to contribute meaningfully to sustainable development by:

  • Empowering communities
  • Contribute to reduce the level of inequality
  • Addressing social ills impacting our people
  • Strive to represent men thus leading to economic development
