Organizational Structure

Mogalakwena Men's Relate (MMR) is governed by a dedicated leadership team committed to upholding the organization's mission and values. Our governance structure ensures transparency, accountability, and effective decision-making to support community development initiatives.

The organization is led by an Executive Committee, which includes a Chairperson, Secretary, Treasurer, and other elected members. This committee oversees strategic planning, financial management, and program implementation, ensuring alignment with our goals of unity and empowerment.

Key Principles

  • Transparency: Open communication and reporting to all stakeholders.
  • Accountability: Responsibility for actions and resources used.
  • Inclusivity: Representation of diverse voices within the community.
  • Sustainability: Long-term planning for enduring impact.
